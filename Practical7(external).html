Nikola Tesla

Pioneer of Electricity | Inventor | Visionary Engineer

The man who shaped modern electricity

About

Nikola Tesla was a brilliant inventor and electrical engineer known for his contributions to the development of alternating current (AC) electrical systems.

Major Contributions

  • Development of AC power system
  • Invention of Tesla Coil
  • Wireless energy transmission concepts
  • Work in electromagnetism and radio

Famous Quote

"The present is theirs; the future, for which I really worked, is mine."
