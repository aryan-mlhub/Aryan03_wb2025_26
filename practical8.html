The Grand Atrium

A Collection of Timeless Masterpieces

The Starry Night

Vincent van Gogh, 1889. A dreamlike depiction of the view from his asylum room in Saint-Rémy-de-Provence.

Mona Lisa

Leonardo da Vinci, c. 1503. Famous for her enigmatic expression and the "sfumato" technique.

The Great Wave

Hokusai, c. 1831. A woodblock print depicting a colossal wave threatening boats off the coast of Kanagawa.

Persistence of Memory

Salvador Dalí, 1931. A surrealist masterpiece featuring melting clocks in a dreamlike landscape.
